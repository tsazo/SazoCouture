About Ed Sazo

Ed Sazo was born in Guatemala City and at the age of six moved to New York City with his parents after the devastating earthquake of 1976.

Being raised in New York, with a family who emphasized on giving back to the community, Ed at a very early age has been active within his church and his neighborhood.

Ed attended Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute to earn his degree in Electrical Engineering. After college, Ed has worked for a variety of companies, improving his abilities as a sales manager and business man.

Professionally, Ed currently works for a technical company, STMicroelectronics, as a sales engineer managing from Canada to Brazil. Additionally, he now tailors custom suits to the perfect fit for each of his customers. After attending classes in New York City, he decided to take his skills to the business world and officially create his start up company Sazo Couture.
